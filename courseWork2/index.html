What we think is important

We are dedicated to providing expectant mothers with the highest quality care and support throughout their pregnancy journey. We understand that pregnancy is a unique and exciting time, and we are committed to helping you achieve a safe and healthy pregnancy and delivery.


Our team of experienced obstetricians and midwives are here to guide you through every step of your pregnancy, from prenatal care and education to labor and delivery. We offer a range of services to ensure that you and your baby receive the best possible care, including regular check-ups, ultrasound scans, and fetal monitoring. Read more about our services.

What a regular proces at our clinic looks like

Hoi

1.

We get in contact

You can check out in this stage if we are the fitting team for you. You will plan a call with you and an appointment.

2.

We decide your stage

We decide what the stage is which you are in in your pregnancy.

3.

We make a program

Based on the outcomes of the previous step, we’ll provide you with a fitting program (check-ups, echo’s etc.) for your pregnancy and situation.

4.

We make a schedule

If you have some medical issues that we should take into account, we will fit the right care in your schedule.

5.

We provide you with a midwife and nurse

After you have met our team and we set up a fitting schedule and the right care for you, you can choose a midwife and maternity nurse.

6.

You'll get regular check ups

When the process at our clinic has started with you, we'll check-up according to schedule. Of course can you always reach us when necessary.

7.

We give you extra help if nessecary

If it seems to be that you need extra care during the pregnancy, we'll adapt your schedule and the care to your needs.

8.

We will contact you after birth

When you gave birth to your little one, we'll have some contact moments afterwards to check in on you and the care you received.
